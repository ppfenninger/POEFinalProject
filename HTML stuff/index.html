Weird Clocks: Project Overview

Inspired by the aesthetic of antique shops with a bunch of kitschy 70s junk, this project unfortunately looks extremely classy, even though some of the decorations are literally from the dump. The project is a three tiered clock that provides a puzzling experience where the user has to correctly trigger all three tiers in the correct order for an exciting surprise. As a team, our objective was to have fun and not kill ourselves. And we did it! We had a lot of fun and didn't die AND made a clockoid puzzle!
